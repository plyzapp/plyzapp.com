The opportunity

The moments brands
have always
overlooked.

Live events create the most receptive audiences in existence — physically present, emotionally engaged, and idle. A festival set break. A stadium halftime. A queue outside the gates. These are not inconveniences. They are the highest-attention windows your brand will ever have — and right now, nobody owns them.

01
The queue
The first impression of your event. 20–45 minutes of anticipation, social energy, and a phone in every hand. The brand that makes this moment enjoyable is the brand people remember inside.
02
The set break
Between headliners, the energy does not drop — it searches for a direction. A branded game on the venue screen or at your stand gives it one. Your brand becomes part of what people talk about that night.
03
The halftime
A stadium at halftime is tens of thousands of competitive, tribally loyal people — still buzzing, not ready to stop. A live leaderboard on the big screen makes your brand the centrepiece of that energy, not a banner in the background.
04
The brand stand
Most activation stands are passed by. A Plyz. game gives people a reason to stop, play, and win something real. The crowd comes to you — and leaves having had an experience with your brand, not just seen a logo.

Platform

Built for live.

Every component engineered for the conditions of a real event — not retrofitted from a marketing tool built for offices and desks.

10-minute campaign setup
Game, brand colours, prize tiers, lead form — all configured in a guided dashboard. No developer. No agency build time. Ready before doors open.
Works on any network
Games are served from edge cache — they load once and run locally on the player's device. Works reliably in a stadium with 50,000 people on shared WiFi.
Instant prize delivery
Prize codes display on-screen the moment the game ends. No email, no wait. The player claims at your bar or stand immediately. Real-time cap management prevents over-redemption.
Live lead dashboard
Every play generates a consent-based lead: name, email, score, timestamp. Access during the event and export at any point. CPL calculation built in.
Two activation formats
Time-boxed competition with a venue-screen leaderboard for set breaks and halftimes. Ambient persistent game with instant probability prizes for queues and brand stands. Choose the format for your context.
Full white-label
Every element — game skin, colours, logo, prize messaging, lead form — customised to your brand. Players never see Plyz. They see your brand at its best.
